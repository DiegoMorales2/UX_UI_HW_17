ABOUT CAMERON MORALES

Cameron Morales is a designer originally from Idalou, TX, with a passion for exploring and creating. Growing up in a small town, Cameron always knew he was destined for something greater. His journey began at 14 when he learned Java and built his first computer, sparking an interest in design and technology. This passion led him to pursue an architecture degree in Santa Monica before returning to Texas to pursue a graphic design degree.

At UTSA, Cameron refined his UX/UI skills, mastering techniques in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and discovering a new hobby in coding. His versatile skill set also extends to cinematography and photography, allowing him to tackle diverse challenges and build amazing websites.

Cameron enjoys going to the gym, golfing, and spending time with friends and family. His love for design and storytelling drives him to create impactful and user-friendly experiences. Whether he's behind the camera or crafting prototypes, Cameron brings creativity and quality to every project.
